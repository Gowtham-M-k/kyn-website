What's a Tribe?

Communities That Fit How You Connect

A Tribe is an interest-based community on Kynhood. Think Discord's structure + Patreon's monetization + Meetup's events — all native in one platform.

🌍

Open Tribe

Free to join, everyone can see and post. Perfect for building a broad community around a shared interest.

e.g. "Chennai Foodies", "Marina Runners"
🔓

Gated Tribe

Free to join, but some content and events are members-only. Freemium model — free tips, premium deep-dives.

e.g. "Fitness Chennai" — free workouts, paid plans
💎

Paid Tribe

Monthly or yearly subscription for full access. Exclusive content, events, and direct connection with the creator.

e.g. "U1NIVERSE Fam — ₹99/mo"
🎫

Event-Born Tribe

Auto-created from event attendees. Everyone who attended U1NIVERSE joins "U1 Fam" — keeping the energy alive long after the concert ends.

🏢

Brand / Sponsor Tribe

Brand-funded, free for users. Companies sponsor communities to build authentic audience relationships. KYN handles setup and moderation tools.
